Energy Professional Services 

Lutha Group is an oil & gas company, originally founded for supporting foreign companies to comply with the new Mexican Energetic regulations and to expand their oil and gas businesses in Mexico. After 1.5 years in the market, with a recently opened and developing energy sector, the company has evolved to become an Energy Commercialization company. Our main purpose is to warranty an exceptional service to our clients through specialized supply chain channels at the best available prices.
